We are Stratagem Africa.

A professional business consultancy committed to serving as a trusted partner for SMEs and investors across Zambia and the African continent.

Our Purpose

Stratagem Africa Advisory was established to bridge the gap between complex regulatory demands and the practical operational needs of growing businesses. We support entrepreneurs by providing clear guidance, accounting systems, and compliance frameworks that unlock growth with confidence.

Rather than acting as simple external service providers, we position ourselves as long-term strategic partners. We are deeply invested in our clients' success, helping them simplify complexity and manage governance requirements securely so they can focus on their core operations.

Our Vision

To be the premier trusted strategic advisory partner for SMEs and cross-border investors in Zambia and across Africa, enabling sustainable corporate growth through sound governance, transparency, and informed strategic guidance.

Our Mission

To support and empower businesses by delivering practical, high-integrity, and client-centric compliance and advisory services that simplify administrative structures and foster long-term commercial success.

Why Stratagem Africa

Building a company in Africa requires navigating distinct local procedures and regulatory agencies. We bring deep, practical compliance knowledge to help you navigate these systems without friction.

  • Strategic partners committed to your long-term success
  • Tailored, SME-focused services for real growth needs
  • Strong local knowledge across PACRA, ZRA, NHIMA, and NAPSA
  • End-to-end integration of company setup and banking advisory
  • Regional, cross-border expertise for foreign market entrants
  • Uncompromising adherence to professional integrity and accountability
