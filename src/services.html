Our Services.

At Stratagem Africa Advisory, we deliver comprehensive corporate compliance, accounting, tax, and strategic market entry solutions to help your business operate with confidence.

01. Company Secretarial & Corporate Compliance

We ensure that your corporate structures are properly established, registered, and maintained in compliance with the laws of Zambia.

Company Registration (PACRA)

  • Corporate name reservation and verification
  • Company incorporation and structuring
  • Directorship allocations and shareholding setup
  • Issuance of all primary statutory registers and documents

Statutory Compliance Management

  • Filing of PACRA Annual Returns
  • Maintenance and updating of statutory registers
  • Processing changes in directorship, shareholding, or capital structure
  • Ongoing corporate governance and compliance advisory

Labour & Social Security Registrations

  • National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIMA) registration
  • National Pension Scheme Authority (NAPSA) registration and monthly compliance setup
Ideal for: Startups, expanding local SMEs, and foreign-owned entities seeking absolute compliance and regulatory peace of mind.

02. Accounting & Bookkeeping

Reliable financial tracking and reporting processes designed to give business owners maximum financial clarity.

Financial Support Solutions

  • Monthly bookkeeping and data entry of transactions
  • Preparation of periodic Management Accounts
  • Monitoring and analysis of business cash flow
  • Regular bank account and ledger reconciliations
  • Expense audits and financial control implementation
  • Liaison support for audits and year-end financial reviews
Ideal for: Growing businesses needing structured financial reporting without the overhead of a full-time in-house accounting department.

03. Taxation & Statutory Filings

We help you navigate the complexity of tax compliance, protecting your business from penalties and audits.

Tax Compliance Offerings

  • Zambia Revenue Authority (ZRA) TPIN registration
  • VAT registration, analysis, and monthly filing support
  • Pay As You Earn (PAYE) calculations and statutory submissions
  • Corporate Income Tax computation and returns filing
  • Comprehensive tax health checks and regulatory advisory
Ideal for: Businesses operating in regulated sectors that require precise tax planning and timely monthly submissions to the ZRA.

04. Corporate Banking Support

Navigating bank requirements for new corporate accounts, especially for international investors and new business owners.

Banking Setup Support

  • Advisory on local commercial bank offerings and terms
  • Compilation and structuring of bank-ready compliance packets
  • Liaison with commercial bank managers to accelerate processes
  • Assistance through multi-currency and corporate setup procedures
Ideal for: International business owners establishing local presence who require expedited, compliant corporate bank account activation.

05. Cross-Border Advisory & Market Entry

Dedicated market entry support for foreign corporations looking to establish commercial operations in Zambia.

Market Entry Solutions

  • Comprehensive market entry feasibility and regulatory guidance
  • Structuring advisory specifically optimized for foreign investors
  • Processing of necessary investment and sector licenses
  • Local representation, setup coordination, and strategic business planning
Ideal for: International companies, NGOs, and regional enterprises executing market entry strategies into Southern Africa.

06. Strategic Business Advisory

Long-term strategic partnership to build business value, optimize operational structures, and plan for scale.

Growth Advisory Solutions

  • Company re-structuring and operations optimization
  • Development of corporate risk management and governance systems
  • Commercial growth strategy and business expansion mapping
  • Structured monthly board advisory partnerships
Ideal for: Mid-sized businesses seeking expert guidance to navigate scaling and secure financing.
